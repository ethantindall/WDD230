About Soda Springs

By Ethan Tindall

Soda Springs. Great town, great people. Great soda. Great springs. Who would have thought? They've got these geysers there apparently, or at least that's what I saw on the pictures online. Maybe they're just really big fountains. Anyways, they've really taken advantage of a great marketing opportunity by selling soda there in town. All soda tastes better in Soda Springs. They've got grape soda, orange soda, lemon lime soda, diet soda, and they serve both Pepsi and Coca-Cola products. Good thing they're not in the midwest or this town would be called Pop Springs. Doesn't have the same ring to it.
