About Fish Haven

By Ethan Tindall

Why is it called Fish Haven, you ask? Well, let me tell you a story. Once upon a time, there was a trapper who was journeying through the wild west and found a nice little place to set up camp along a river. He pulled out his fishing pole, cast a line and sat back waiting for bites. A good while had passed and he still hadn't caught anything, so he put his hand over his brow and looked out over the river. He could see the fish swimming around out there having a good time and realized they were so full from eating all the bugs and whatnot around that they weren't interested in his bait. "Well," he thought to himself. "This must be the place to be if you're a fish." And voila, Fish Haven was born.
