Fredoka One:

Fredoka One is a bold, bouncy that reminds one of clouds. The large, sans-serif letters help those with poor eyesight better navigate the information on the page.

Jost:

Jost is another sans-serif font. This one is more reserved and professional looking. It serves to present the information in a simple, straightforward manner, while Fredoka One helps attract the user's attention.
