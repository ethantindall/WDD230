The colors I have chosen for my website are bright pastel colors. The font will be black, and the background will be light yellow. This creates contrast which helps those with poor eyesight to more easily interpret the information they are being presented.

The logo is a bright yellow sun, and the sight will be highlighted by blue, green, and melon accents. These simple pastels give a more modern, minimalistic feel to the sight. This lightweight design concept will help users find information more easily.
