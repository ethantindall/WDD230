About Preston

By Ethan Tindall

Preston, Preston, Preston. I don't know much about Preston. Nobody does really, it's a mysterious village in the middle of the Idaho wilderness. Rumor has it that the citizens spend their days training in Rex-kwon-do and watching MTV. Once, my cousin Denny visited Preston and he said they had great hamburgers though. Besides that the only thing in town worth mentioning is the thrift shop. You can get all sorts of cool stuff there. He found some cool slightly used ninja equipment. On an unrelated note, I think I'm going to watch Napoleon Dynamite tonight.
